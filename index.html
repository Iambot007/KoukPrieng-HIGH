About Our School

Our Mission

At NGS Kouk Prieng High School, we are committed to providing quality education that empowers students to achieve academic excellence while developing crucial life skills. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who will become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

We envision a learning environment where every student receives personalized attention and guidance to discover their unique potential. Our goal is to establish NGS Kouk Prieng High School as a center of educational excellence in Svay Rieng Province and beyond.
